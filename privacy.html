Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

Short version: we don't ask for much, we don't sell your data, and the ads on this site are served by Google. The details below explain exactly what that means.

Who we are

Trust Me Bro ("we", "us") is a community tier-list site at qafizz.com. This policy covers that site. If you don't agree with it, please stop using the site.

What we collect

Information you give us

If you create an account, we store the display name and email address you provide, plus the content you post — tier lists, votes, and comments. That content is public by design. Don't put anything in a tier list or comment that you wouldn't want the internet to see.

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, our hosting and analytics infrastructure records standard technical data when you visit:

  • IP address and approximate region
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages viewed, time of visit, and referring site

We use this to keep the site running, understand which tier lists are popular, and detect abuse such as vote manipulation.

Local storage

We store a small amount of data in your browser (via localStorage) to remember your theme preference and your session. This stays on your device and is not transmitted to us as a cookie.

Advertising and cookies

This site displays ads served through Google AdSense. You should understand the following:

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits to our site and/or other sites on the internet.
  • Third-party vendors and ad networks may also use cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies to collect information about your browsing activity for ad targeting and measurement.
  • We do not control these third-party cookies and do not have access to the data they collect.

How to opt out

  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can opt out of third-party vendor cookies for personalized advertising at aboutads.info/choices or optout.networkadvertising.org.
  • Most browsers let you block or delete cookies in their settings. Doing so won't break the site, though ads may become less relevant.

For more detail on how Google uses data when you use partner sites, see Google's policy page.

How we use your information

  • To operate the site and display the content you asked for
  • To maintain your account and session
  • To prevent spam, vote manipulation, and abuse
  • To measure traffic and understand what people find interesting
  • To display advertising, which is how the site pays for itself

We do not sell your personal information.

Data retention

Account information is kept for as long as your account exists. Public content you post may remain visible after account deletion, though it will be disassociated from your account. Technical logs are retained for a limited period for security and troubleshooting.

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete the personal information we hold about you, and to object to certain processing. This includes rights under the GDPR (if you're in the EU/EEA or UK), the CCPA (California), and PIPEDA (Canada). To exercise any of these, contact us using the details below and we'll respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we'll remove it.

Third-party links

Tier lists and comments may link to other sites. We're not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites — read their policies yourself.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we hold, but no website can promise perfect security. Use a unique password, and don't reuse one from another service.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the site changes. Material changes will be reflected in the "last updated" date at the top of this page. Continuing to use the site after a change means you accept it.
