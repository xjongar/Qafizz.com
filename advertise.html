Ads keep this site free. Here's how they work.

Trust Me Bro is supported entirely by advertising. There's no subscription, no paywall, and no plan to add one.

How ads run here

Advertising on this site is served programmatically through Google AdSense. If you want to reach our audience, the simplest route is a Google Ads campaign targeting sites in our category — that inventory is available to any advertiser through the auction.

For direct placements or sponsorships, email us using the details below.

Placements

  • Leaderboard (728×90) — above the fold, first thing on the page
  • Sidebar rectangle (300×250) — top of the sidebar, alongside the trending feed
  • Sticky skyscraper (300×600) — follows the reader down the sidebar; our highest-viewability slot
  • In-content (728×90) — between a tier list and its comments, where engagement is highest
  • In-feed — inline units within the tier list feed
  • Mobile anchor (320×50) — dismissible, pinned to the bottom on mobile

Our approach

Every slot reserves its height before the ad loads, so content never jumps under the reader. Units fill lazily as they come into view rather than all at once. The mobile anchor can be dismissed. We'd rather show fewer ads that people actually see than carpet the page with ones they don't.

Audience

Readers come here to rank and argue about consumer culture — food, games, sport, film, music, tech, and internet ephemera. It's an engaged, opinionated audience that arrives with intent rather than by accident.

We're a young site and growing. If you want current traffic figures before committing to a direct buy, ask and we'll share them honestly.

What we won't run

  • Malware, deceptive downloads, or fake system warnings
  • Adult content
  • Ads designed to look like site content or navigation
  • Anything that auto-plays sound
